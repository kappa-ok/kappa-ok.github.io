So Who Am I?

I'm kappa. I'm a game, web, and software developer from Canada
But that doesn't tell you much, does it?
I've always had a passion for creation - from doodling silly characters to writing novels,
I'm always making something. After a few years of working on my drawing and animation skills,
I heard about this indie game called Undertale. After watching some videos, I decided to get it
and play it for myself. This ended up making me realize that not all games are just mindlessly
mowing down zombies, while using cool guns. Undertale has planted the idea in my single braincell
that games can be interactive stories. And from there, I spiraled. Now, I'm a few lines of Lua away
from completing an action RPG called Moth Warrior. And that's not where I'm stopping. There's so
many ideas floating through my tiny brain that I just *have* to make. So that's my backstory.
Hopefully this helps you know me better.
